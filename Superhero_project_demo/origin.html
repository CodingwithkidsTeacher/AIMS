ORIGIN STORY

Alex Rivera was an ordinary teenager who spent most nights coding in his school’s computer lab. One evening, while testing a powerful experimental AI system, something suddenly went terribly wrong. The machine overloaded and exploded into waves of glowing blue pixel energy. As the digital particles surrounded him, Alex discovered he had gained incredible powers connected to the digital world itself. From that moment on, he became Captain Pixel — the protector of the internet and humanity’s last defense against cyber chaos.
