PIXEL BATTLE

Captain Pixel raced across the rooftops of Neon City as giant glitch robots attacked civilians below. Laser blasts exploded through the night sky while drones swarmed overhead. Using his glowing pixel shield, Captain Pixel blocked enemy attacks and fought back with incredible speed and power. Even against impossible odds, he refused to surrender. The future of the internet depended on this battle.
