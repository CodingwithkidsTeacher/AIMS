DR. LAG RETURNS

Dr. Lag returned stronger and more dangerous than ever before. Using advanced cyber weapons, he hacked into the city's technology systems and spread chaos across Neon City. Giant holograms flashed warning messages while drones filled the skies. Citizens panicked as the city spiraled out of control. But Captain Pixel knew he had to stop Dr. Lag before the digital world collapsed forever.
